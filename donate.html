Donors


Become a part of our efforts to educate, inspire, and protect!


Seeing an exotic animal up close is both an educational and visceral experience – you see how they move, you hear a roar or a snort, and sometimes you can even smell them. You learn first-hand how important saving these creatures from extinction is, and helping our Zoo visitors to develop this environmental awareness, knowledge, and empathy is a top priority for us.


But we can’t do this important work without your support.


The Greater Los Angeles Zoo Association (GLAZA) offers a variety of donor programs and opportunities that help us inspire our visitors, support species around the world, and provide you with some wonderful benefits. Be a part of something special and support this amazing community resource. See below for all of the ways to support your Los Angeles Zoo.
